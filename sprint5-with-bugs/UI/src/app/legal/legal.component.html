ESG Compliance Statement

ToolShop is committed to full compliance with all applicable environmental, social, and governance (ESG) regulations and standards. We adhere to relevant legal and regulatory requirements related to environmental protection, labor practices, data privacy, and corporate governance.

Our ESG compliance statements are reviewed annually and are available to all stakeholders. We maintain transparent and accountable business practices, including regular ESG performance reporting, an independent ethics hotline, and a Board-level ESG committee to oversee our sustainability strategy.

By engaging with our products and services, you acknowledge our commitment to ESG compliance. For any legal inquiries, please contact us.
