Our Sustainability & ESG Commitment

ESG Mission Statement

At ToolShop, we are committed to operating our business in an environmentally responsible, socially conscious, and ethically governed manner. Our mission is to provide high-quality tools while minimizing our environmental footprint, supporting our communities, and upholding the highest standards of corporate governance.

We believe that sustainable business practices are not only the right thing to do — they are essential to our long-term success and to the well-being of the planet we share.

Company Vision

Our vision is to be the leading sustainable tool retailer, delivering innovative products that empower professionals and DIY enthusiasts while contributing positively to society and the environment. We aim to achieve net-zero carbon emissions by 2040 and to source 100% of our materials from certified sustainable suppliers by 2030.

Environmental Commitment

  • Reducing packaging waste by 50% by 2026 through recyclable and biodegradable materials.
  • Partnering with suppliers who adhere to ISO 14001 environmental management standards.
  • Offering a tool repair and refurbishment program to extend product lifecycles.
  • Offsetting all shipping-related carbon emissions through certified carbon offset programs.

Social Responsibility

  • Fair wages and safe working conditions across our entire supply chain.
  • Supporting local communities through charitable donations and volunteering programs.
  • Promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion within our workforce.
  • Providing vocational training and scholarships for students in technical fields.

Governance

We maintain transparent and accountable business practices, including regular ESG performance reporting, an independent ethics hotline, and a Board-level ESG committee to oversee our sustainability strategy.

Our ESG compliance statements are reviewed annually and are available to all stakeholders. We adhere to applicable legal and regulatory requirements related to environmental and social governance.
